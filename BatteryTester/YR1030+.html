Battery Tester YR1030+

The YR1030+ is a precise measuring device for measuring the voltage and internal resistance of lithium (Li-Ion, LiFePO4, LiMn, LiPo), NiMH & NiCd and lead batteries as single cells or battery packs up to a maximum of 28V. The measurement and long-term monitoring of the internal resistance allows a convenient quality check of new batteries and also a simple documentation of ageing in long-term use. With the battery tester you can check freshly delivered batteries and sort out inferior batteries.
