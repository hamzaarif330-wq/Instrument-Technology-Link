Transformer Turn Ratio Tester ES 6030

The Fuzrr ES 6030 Transformer Turn Ratio Meter is a professional-grade TTR meter designed for accurate measurement of transformer turn ratios. With a wide variable ratio range of 0.9 to 10000, this instrument is suitable for testing single-phase transformers, three-phase transformers, Z-type transformers, potential transformers (PT), and current transformers (CT).
