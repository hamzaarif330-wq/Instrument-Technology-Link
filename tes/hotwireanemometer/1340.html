Hotwire Anemometer TES 1340

The TES 1340 is a precision hot wire anemometer (thermal anemometer) designed to accurately measure air velocity, airflow (CFM/CMM), and temperature, utilizing hot wire technology that provides fast response and highly precise measurements even at low air velocities where traditional vane anemometers are ineffective. This professional air velocity meter features multiple selectable units (m/s, ft/min, knots, km/h, mph), telescopic probe for reaching into ducts and tight spaces, large LCD display with backlight for clear visibility, data hold function to freeze readings, max/min/average recording for trend analysis, and auto power-off to conserve battery. The TES 1340 enables HVAC technicians, clean room certification specialists, laboratory researchers, and industrial hygienists to perform low-flow duct traversing, verify clean room airflow (ISO 14644), test laminar flow hoods, measure fume hood face velocity, inspect ventilation systems, and conduct critical airflow analysis.
