Concrete/Wood Moisture Meter MS 7002

The Lutron MS-7002 is a high-precision digital moisture meter featuring wide measurement ranges (wood 6-40%, concrete 0-100% relative, gypsum 0.2-50%), large LCD display, data hold, max/min memory, separate 2-pin electrode probe for direct surface measurement, RS-232 interface for PC connectivity, auto power-off, and compact durable design for fast, reliable moisture detection in construction and quality control applications.
